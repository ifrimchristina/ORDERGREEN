FAQ

What is ordergreen?

Ordergreen is an online shop that aims to connects customers to smaller businesses and farmer markets and to be able to deliver the purchased produce through environment-friendly means.

What is our mission?

Our official mission statement is to be able to connect customers to the farmer markets and smaller business through our website. We want to be able to deliver your purchase with little to no carbon footprint.

What is the delivery time?

It takes around 50 minutes to 2 hours depending on where you live.
